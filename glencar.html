Glencar Waterfall Trips

What better way to spend some time in the great out doors then on a short hike around Glencar Waterfall,Fureys Cabs & Bus hire provide trips to Glencar Waterfall every 2nd Saturday of the month, but places fill up fast so make sure to book early to avoid disappointment Book Now.

The Glencar Waterfall trip includes:

  • Transport
    • Pick up 9am at the cresent Boyle
    • Leaving Glencar Waterfall at 1:00pm sharp for lunch in Yeats Tavern
    • Return - Leaving Yeats Tavern at 2:30pm sharp
  • Food
    • Lunch
    • 1 x Tea or Coffee

Ticket Admission package prices include above food, transport and ticket entry

Packages are as follows:

  • Package 1 - € 20.00
    • 1 x Adult
    • 1 x Kid
  • Package 2 - € 40.00
    • 2 x Adult
    • 2 x Kids under 10
