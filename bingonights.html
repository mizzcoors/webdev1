Bingo Night Trips

If you fancy your luck at a bit of Bingo,Fureys Cabs & Bus hire provide transport to Bingo Nights every Monday to Drumshambo, but places fill up fast so make sure to book early to avoid disappointment Book Now.

The Bingo Night trips includes:

  • Transport
    • Pick up 8pm at the cresent Boyle
    • Return - Leaving Bingo hall at 10:00pm sharp

Pricing to Drumshambo hall is as follows:

  • 1 x Adult-Return € 10.00
  • 1 x Teenager-Return € 5.00

For larger groups or alternative bingo venues etc. please feel free to Contact Us
