Dublin Zoo Trips

What better way to spend some time with the kids then a day at Dublin Zoo,Fureys Cabs & Bus hire provide trips to Dublin every 3rd Saturday of the month, but places fill up fast so make sure to book early to avoid disappointment Book Now.

The Dublin Zoo trip includes:

  • Transport
    • Pick up 8am at the cresent Boyle
    • Return - Leaving Dublin Zoo at 4:30pm sharp
  • Food
    • Lunch
    • 1 x Tea or Coffee

Ticket Admission package prices include above food, transport and ticket entry

Packages are as follows:

  • Package 1 - € 50.00
    • 1 x Adult
    • 1 x Kid
  • Package 2 - € 100.00
    • 2 x Adult
    • 2 x Kids under 10
